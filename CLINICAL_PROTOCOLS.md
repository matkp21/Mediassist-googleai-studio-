# Clinical Protocols & Institutional Guidelines

This document serves as the "Master Truth" for local medical protocols. AI agents in Pro Mode refer to this file to ensure compliance with institutional standards.

## 1. Antibiotic Stewardship
- **First-line for Uncomplicated UTI**: Nitrofurantoin 100mg MR twice daily for 3 days (Women) or 7 days (Men).
- **Sepsis**: Immediate escalation to Broad-Spectrum (e.g., Piperacillin/Tazobactam) within 1 hour ("The Golden Hour").

## 2. Diabetic Management
- **HbA1c Target**: 48 mmol/mol (7.0%) for most adults with type 2 diabetes.
- **Hypoglycemia Protocol**: 15g of fast-acting glucose, re-check in 15 mins.

## 3. Documentation Standards
- **SOAP Note Required**: Every patient interaction must be synthesized into Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan.
- **Identity Integrity**: All clinical notes must link to a verified Physician ID.

## 4. AI-Safety Rules (Inspired by Goose/Nanobot)
- **Dosage Verification**: Any drug dosage suggested by AI MUST be cross-referenced with the local formulary tool.
- **HITL (Human-In-The-Loop)**: AI cannot directly commit to the EHR; all actions require signed physician approval.
