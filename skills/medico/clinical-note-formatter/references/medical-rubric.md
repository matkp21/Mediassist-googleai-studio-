# Institutional Clinical Documentation Rubric
## Overview
All SOAP notes must strictly adhere to the following structure. Any omission of critical vitals or unsupported assessments will be scored heavily against.

### 1. Subjective (S)
- Chief Complaint (CC) in the patient's own words.
- History of Present Illness (HPI) using OLD CARTS (Onset, Location, Duration, Character, Aggravating/Alleviating factors, Radiation, Timing, Severity).
- Relevant PMHx, PSHx, Meds, Allergies.

### 2. Objective (O)
- Vital Signs (T, HR, BP, RR, SpO2).
- Physical Exam findings, localized and specific.
- Diagnostic lab results or imaging.

### 3. Assessment (A)
- 1 sentence summary statement.
- Primary working diagnosis with reasoning.
- Top 2-3 Differential Diagnoses (with ruling out/in rationale).

### 4. Plan (P)
- Diagnostics (What to test next).
- Therapeutics (Meds, procedures).
- Patient Education.
- Follow up.
