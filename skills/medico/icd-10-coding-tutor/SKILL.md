# ICD-10 Coding Tutor

## Purpose
Presents a clinical scenario and guides the student through the logic of selecting the correct WHO billing codes, practicing administrative skills essential for clinical practice.

## Progressive Disclosure workflow
Connects to WHO ICD-10 database via MCP. The massive taxonomy of codes is only queried directly by this sub-agent and isolated from the rest of the application context.
