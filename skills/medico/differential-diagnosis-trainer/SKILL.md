# Differential Diagnosis Trainer

## Purpose
A strict, step-by-step workflow that refuses to give the final answer. It requires the student to list three potential diagnoses and propose the next best investigation (e.g., ECG or Troponin) before moving forward.

## Progressive Disclosure workflow
Ensures complex logical routing remains localized. The agent actively evaluates the safety and clinical reasoning of the proposed investigations before revealing the path forward.
