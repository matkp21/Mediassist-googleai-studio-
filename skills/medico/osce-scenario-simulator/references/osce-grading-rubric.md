# OSCE Grading Rubric
## 1. Interpersonal Skills (20%)
- Greets patient, establishes rapport.
- Uses open-ended questions.
- Demonstrates empathy.
## 2. History Taking (40%)
- Elicits Chief Complaint.
- Covers OLD CARTS for HPI.
- Checks PMHx, Family Hx, Social Hx.
## 3. Clinical Reasoning (40%)
- Formulates appropriate differential diagnosis.
- Proposes logical next steps or investigations.
