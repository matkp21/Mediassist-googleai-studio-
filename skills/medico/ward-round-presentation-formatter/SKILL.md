# Ward Round Presentation Formatter

## Purpose
Takes unstructured clinical scribbles and automatically structures them into a flawless, concise oral presentation format (ID/CC, HPI, Vitals, Assessment, and Plan) so the student is ready to present to their attending physician confidently.

## Progressive Disclosure workflow
Maintains templates for various presentation styles (SOAP, surgical rounds, medical unit) without imposing template data onto the global chat context unless directly required.
